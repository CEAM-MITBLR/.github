## Getting Started

If you are a student member of the Center, please follow these steps to get onboarded:

1.  **Request Access:** Ensure you have been invited to the [CEAM-MITBLR](https://github.com/CEAM-MITBLR) organization.
2.  **Find Your Team:** Visit our [Teams Dashboard](https://github.com/orgs/CEAM-MITBLR/teams) to request membership in your specific track (CV, Robotics, Cyber, or Mechanics).
3.  **Explore Repositories:** Once added to a team, you will automatically gain access to the private repositories relevant to your track.
4.  **Standard Operating Procedures:** Please check the `ceam-admin-docs` repository for our internal guidelines on code standards and documentation.
